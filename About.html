About Us

About us: Offline started August 17, 2016. Kalki Fashion Design provides only offline tailoring services in Perundurai (638052).

The new generation of women has a great fashion sense and spends a lot of time finding a professional tailor.

Kalki Fashion Design is an exclusive experience that offers you and conveys to you a peculiar logic of design apart from whatever style it may be, and we deliver with the perfect stitch and as per our promise.

We have a very expert and professional tailoring team that offers you custom-made clothing to integrate your uniqueness into your clothing. They are then set to measure.

The key advantage of custom-tailored clothing or an outfit is that it will fit you like a glove, and for sure, you will love it.

Custom stitch saree blouses, salwar suits, anarkali suits, kurti tops, lehenga, and ethnic bottomwear in styles and fits that complement you perfectly! When you choose to stitch clothes that suit you, both personality-wise and fit-wise, you end up with the most amazing set of clothes that speak to your style!
